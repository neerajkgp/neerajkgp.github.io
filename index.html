About Me

I am currently working as a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at Adobe, focusing on cutting-edge research and applications in NLP, Computer Vision, and Multimodal AI. I completed my PhD at IIT Delhi, where my thesis explored the impact of normalization in Multimodal AI, and hold a B-Tech in Electronics and Electrical Communication Engineering from IIT Kharagpur (2014).

My research has been published in top-tier conferences and journals including IEEE TAI, IEEE TASL, ECML PKDD, Interspeech, ICONIP, IJCAI, ITU J-FET, and CVPR. I hold 10 patents in AI and machine learning, and actively contribute to the research community as a reviewer and mentor.
